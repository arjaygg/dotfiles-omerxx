## Common Mistakes to Avoid

| Wrong | Correct |
|---|---|
| `searchForPattern({ pattern: "..." })` | `searchForPattern({ substring_pattern: "..." })` |
| `searchForPattern({ lines_before: 5 })` | `searchForPattern({ context_lines_before: 5 })` |
| `searchForPattern({ lines_after: 5 })` | `searchForPattern({ context_lines_after: 5 })` |
| `get_function_details({ function_name: "Serena.X" })` | `get_function_details({ functions: ["Serena.X"] })` |
